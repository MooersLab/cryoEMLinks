Still a work in progress. Please report dead links, corrections, and suggested additions to blaine-mooers at ouhsc.edu.

First deployed on GitHubPages on 4 November 2020. Much updating still planned. Last updated 5 November 2020.

Acronyms:

  • CCC: cross correleation coefficient
  • CTF: contrast transfer function
  • EER: electron event representation
  • FSC: Fourier Shell Correlation
  • SCF: Sampling Compensation Factor
  • SSNR: spectral signal-to-noise ratio
